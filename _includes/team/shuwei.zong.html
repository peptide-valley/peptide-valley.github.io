Shuwei Zong
Founding Partner

Mr. Zong started his career in sales in the fields of Communication, Automobile, Real Estate. He has served as General Manager, Director and Chairman in many Chinese corporations.

A serial entrepreneur, Mr. Zong co-founded Jilin TaiGu (Peptide) Bioengineering Co., Ltd. in 2012. He founded SMPV in 2017 and has served as Chairman and General Manager since the company's inception.

Mr. Zong is the Executive Director of Jilin Charity Federation and the first Director of Jilin Provincial Traditional Chinese Medicine Health Industry Association.
