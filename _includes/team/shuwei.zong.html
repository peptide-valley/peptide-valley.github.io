Shuwei Zong
Founding Partner
tiger@peptide-valley.com

Mr. Zong started his career in sales in the fields of Communication, Automobile, and Real Estate. He has served as General Manager, Director and Chairman in many Chinese corporations.

A serial entrepreneur, Mr. Zong co-founded Jilin TaiGu (Peptide Valley) Bioengineering Co., Ltd. in 2012. He founded SMPV in 2017 and has been serving as its Chairman and General Manager ever since.

Mr. Zong is the Executive Director of Jilin Charity Federation and the first Director of Jilin Provincial Traditional Chinese Medicine Health Industry Association.
