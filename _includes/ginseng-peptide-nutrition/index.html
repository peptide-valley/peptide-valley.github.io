Ginseng Peptide Nutrition

Panax ginseng C. A. Meyer (ginseng) has been the most widely used and acclaimed herb in Chinese communities for thousands of years. It has wide range of biological and pharmacological activities and contains various active and nutritional ingredients.

The main bioactive substances in ginseng are polysaccharides, volatile oils, flavonoids, peptides, amino acids, vitamins, organic acids, fatty acids, and trace elements.

With the rapid development of biochemistry and molecular biology technology, the research of ginseng oligopeptide, due to its unique physicochemical properties, gradually attracted the attention of researchers.
