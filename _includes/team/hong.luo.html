Hong Luo
Founding Partner

Ms. Luo has a broad educational background from undergraduate degree in Aerospace Engineering, master degree in Sociology, Mass Communication and Social Influence, to an MBA from the Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania.

She has worked as an Aerospace Engineer, senior consultant at Bain & Company. She founded Chinese Culture Media Company and produced many TV programs for STAR Xing Kong, MTV, and BBC. She is leading the Retirement sector in Shenzhen Neptunus Group Co Ltd.
